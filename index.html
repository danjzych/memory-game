Welcome to The Memory Game

Do you trust what you see? The Memory Game will challenge your memory.

Playing the game is simple, winning is not. To play, flip two cards to see if they match.

If they do match, they will stay face-up. If not, they will flip back over. Try to remember what colors were where.

To begin, simply select your difficult. If you want your best score tracked, leave your name as well.
